At a high level, a constable may enter and search premises for the following purposes:

  - Pursusing when unlawfully at large (arrest)
  - Life & Limb / serious damage
  - Escaped lawful custody
  - Arrest for indictable offence
  - Statutory offences e.g. POA, Drink Drive, Fail to stop
  - Executing a warrant
