A constable has the power to search a person who has been arrested if reasonable grounds for believing the arrested person may be:

  - Danger to themselves or others
  - Concealed on them any implement they might use to assist them to escape from lawful custody
  - Concealed on them anything that might be evidence.

Applies only to arrests made in a place other than a police station.  When searching in public, search is limited to removal of Jacket, Outer Garment, Gloves.

Dont forget the following too:

  - PACE, Section 18 (1): Entry and search following custody
  - PACE, Section 18 (5): Entry and search before custody
