Anything seized for the purposes of a criminal investigation may be retained - for use as evidence at a trial for an offence; or for forensic examination or for investigation in connection with an offence; and anything may be retained in order to establish its lawful owner, where there are reasonable grounds for believing that it has been obtained in consequence of the commission of an offence
